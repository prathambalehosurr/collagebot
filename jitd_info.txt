Completion of 10+2/PUC with Physics and Mathematics as compulsory subjects
One optional subject: Chemistry/Biology/Biotechnology/Computer Science/Electronics
Minimum 45% aggregate marks (40% for SC/ST/OBC candidates)
For MBA:

Bachelor's degree with at least 50% marks from a recognized university
Valid entrance exam score (PGCET/KMAT/CAT/CMAT/XAT/MAT/ATMA/GMAT)
